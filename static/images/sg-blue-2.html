    Product Description
    Immersive Display

    Watch movies, videos, and TV shows come alive on the 16.63 cm (6.5) Infinity-O display of this Samsung smartphone. Its wide aspect ratio offers an immersive viewing experience, every single time.

    Quad-camera Setup

    This smartphone comes with a high-resolution quad-camera setup, featuring a 48 MP main camera, an 8 MP ultra-wide camera, a 2 MP depth camera, and a 2 MP macro camera, so you can capture amazing moments of your life and surroundings.

    Ultra-wide-angle Camera

    Thanks to the 8 MP ultra-angle-wide camera, with a 123° field of vision, this smartphone lets you capture a larger view of mountains, landscapes, and cityscapes.

    Macro Camera

    This smartphone’s 2 MP macro camera helps you capture stunning close-up shots with amazing clarity. You can even blur the background in order to isolate your subject so it stands out.

    Depth Camera

    With the 2 MP depth camera, you can make your subject the sole focus of a picture, while reducing background noise and blurring out the background. You can also adjust the depth of field when you’re clicking a picture and even after you have captured it.

    Amazing Selfies

    Nail the perfect selfie by getting this smartphone from Samsung. The 13 MP front camera helps you capture stunning and high-resolution selfies. You can use the Live Focus effects to subtly blur the background so your face stands out.

    Long-lasting Battery

    With a 5000 mAh battery, this smartphone lets you enjoy up to 21 hours of video playback time. Also, if you get a low-battery notification, this smartphone supports 15 W of fast charging so you can get your phone charged up and ready in a jiffy.

    Powerful Processor

    Say hello to fast processing speeds and spacious storage, with the Samsung Galaxy A21s. Featuring an octa-core processor and 6 GB of RAM, this smartphone helps you perform tasks seamlessly and multitask like a pro. Also, you can save photos, videos, and more on this smartphone, thanks to the 64 GB of internal storage. The microSD card slot, which is expandable up to 512 GB, helps you save all your favorite movies and files in this single device.

    Stunning Design

    With a glossy and holographic finish, this smartphone from Samsung is sure to grab the attention of onlookers for all the right reasons. This smartphone’s smooth curves provide a firm grip so you can hold this phone with ease.
